title: Cyber Lux
category: UI/UX Design
year: 2024
tags: Figma, UI Design, iGaming, Accessibility, Design System

Cyber Lux is a futuristic online casino concept created as a UI/UX showcase. Designed in neon purple and pink tones, it delivers a premium cyber-night aesthetic inspired by digital luxury and technology.
The interface features smooth animations, glowing elements, and a clear content hierarchy for intuitive navigation. A built-in Accessibility panel allows users to adjust contrast, text size, readability, and interaction settings  ensuring an inclusive experience without compromising the visual style.
This is a fully fictional concept project demonstrating a modern, immersive, and accessible casino experience.
