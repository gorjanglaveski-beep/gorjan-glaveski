title: Appendix Jungle Pilsner
category: Brand Identity
year: 2023
tags: Illustrator, Brand Design, Packaging, Typography, Print

Appendix Jungle Pilsner is a craft beer concept inspired by the contrast between raw nature and urban rhythm. Built on a classic pilsner foundation, the brand expresses a bold, organic, and contemporary visual identity.

Jungle is not a place, but a mindset  a symbol of instinct, freedom, and independent spirit. The design combines strong typography, a restrained color palette, and raw textures to create an authentic, unpolished character aimed at those who seek something beyond the mainstream.
