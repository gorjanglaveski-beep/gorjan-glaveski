title: Nuna
category: Brand Identity
year: 2023
tags: Illustrator, Brand Design, Logo, Typography, Packaging

NUNA is a mini brand inspired by my personal love for pasta. The concept represents “street pasta to go” spaghetti with an urban character, blending the taste of tradition with the fast-paced rhythm of modern living. With a clean and creative design, NUNA brings pasta to the streets as a modern culinary experience.
