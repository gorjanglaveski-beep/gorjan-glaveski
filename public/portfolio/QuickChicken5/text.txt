title: Quick Chicken
category: Brand Identity
year: 2023
tags: Illustrator, Brand Design, Logo, Packaging, Food & Beverage

Quick Chicken is a modern fast-food brand focused on high quality chicken nuggets, created for people who want a quick, tasty meal with a consistent standard. The brand combines a simple menu with a recognizable visual identity and youthful energy, positioning itself as a go to choice for fast food without compromising on taste. The project explores the balance between speed, quality, and the playful character of a contemporary street food brand.
